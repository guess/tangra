Consent

Before you can participate in this study, you must give out your consent to the investigator. You must agree to what is written in this document.

First and foremost, although the website keeps track of your data, they will never be shared outside from our laboratory. When we use your data for study and for publishing, they will be anonymized so that an outside party cannot trace you.

Secondly, this experiment should pose no risk to you whatsoever. However, since the experiment is done on computer, you may experience physical discomfort associated with general usage of computer.

If you agree to these terms and want to continue on with the study, please click Proceed. You may withdraw now from the study without any penalty. You may also withdraw at any time except during the stage where you need to play the game. This is to ensure the integrity of the data.
